OSA’S FOUNDING PRINCIPLES

MISSION STATEMENT:

The mission of OSA, a non-profit 501(c)(6), is to unite and grow American soccer by building an authentic and affordable multi-tiered alliance of professional and amateur clubs and leagues who advocate for an open system that reflects the sport’s structure globally.

VISION STATEMENT:

The vision of OSA is to create a national multi-tiered alliance of clubs and leagues controlled by its member teams that embrace the open system principles of sporting merit, solidarity, promotion and relegation, membership control and territorial freedom.

OSA
PILLARS:

The pillars of OSA are working with and supporting leagues and clubs that advocate open system tenets in a structure that offers significant prize money, reasonable revenue sharing, regional travel and affordable membership fees and annual dues.
